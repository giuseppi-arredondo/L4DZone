FAQ for L4DZone

What is L4DZone?

L4DZone is a web platform designed to organize competitive Left 4 Dead 2 matches. Here, you'll find advanced tools for custom configurations, ranking systems, and exclusive features that enhance the overall gaming experience for all users.

What do I need to play?

  • Playtime: At least 100 hours in L4D2.
  • Versus Matches: At least 50 matches completed.
  • Basic Versus Knowledge:
    • Know how to attack with each infected.
    • Know the routes of the maps.
    • Teamwork skills.
  • Original Game: No pirated versions.
  • Steam without Family Sharing.

How to play on L4DZone?

  1. Register and link your Steam account.
  2. Accept rules.
  3. Download custom maps from "Maps".
  4. Click "Play Queue".
  5. When IP appears:
    • Connect with the IP.
    • Type !ready.

What if I leave a match?

Leaving causes penalties from 1 hour to 32 days.

  • If you don’t !ready or connect in 5 minutes → penalty.
  • After match starts:
    • 5 minutes max total inactivity.
    • 4 minutes per disconnection.

MMR System

Win = +MMR. Lose = -MMR. Large score differences = more effect.

Servers

Optimized servers with region selection for best ping.

Support

  • Report Players: Use the form.
  • Help: Ask via Discord or live support.

Extras

Type !ready always. See “Rules” and “Maps” to get started.
