FAQ for L4DZone

What is L4DZone?

L4DZone is a web platform designed to organize competitive Left 4 Dead 2 matches. Here, you'll find advanced tools for custom configurations, ranking systems, and exclusive features that enhance the overall gaming experience for all users.

What do I need to play?

  • Playtime: Have at least 100 hours played in Left 4 Dead 2.
  • Versus Matches: Have completed at least 50 matches in Versus mode.
  • Basic Versus Knowledge:
    • Knowing how to spawn/respawn and attack with each infected class.
    • Familiarity with routes to the saferoom on the official maps.
    • Effective teamwork.
  • Original Copy of the Game: A legitimate version of Left 4 Dead 2 is required.
  • Steam Account without Family Sharing: Accounts with Family Sharing enabled are not allowed.

How to play on L4DZone?

  1. Register on our website and validate your Steam account.
  2. Accept the platform's rules.
  3. Make sure you have downloaded the custom maps from the "Maps" section.
  4. Click the "Play Queue" button to join the match queue.
  5. Once the server's IP is displayed:
    • Connect to the server using the provided IP.
    • Type !ready in the in-game chat to confirm your participation.
    • Enjoy the match!

Note: A sound notification will alert you when the queue is full. By default, the volume is set to maximum, but you can adjust it as needed.

What happens if I leave a match?

Leaving a match disrupts the experience for other players and results in temporary penalties. Penalty durations range from 1 hour to 32 days, depending on your recent match history.

  • If you fail to type !ready or join the server within 5 minutes after the IP is displayed, the match will be canceled, and you will receive a penalty.
  • After the match starts, the inactivity limits are:
    • 5 minutes of total inactivity per match.
    • 4 minutes per period of inactivity or disconnection.

The first limit reached will be applied.

How does the MMR system work?

The MMR (Matchmaking Rating) system is used to balance competitive matches:

  • Gain and Loss: Winning matches increases your MMR, while losing reduces it.
  • Score Influence: The point difference between teams impacts the amount of MMR gained or lost.
  • Visible Ranks: You must complete at least 10 matches for your rank to become visible.

L4DZone Servers

L4DZone operates optimized, proprietary servers to ensure smooth gameplay. Servers are automatically assigned based on your geographic region to prioritize performance.

How to report or contact support?

  • Report Players: Use the "Report" form to notify inappropriate behavior or technical issues.
  • Additional Support: Reach out to our team via live support or through our official Discord group.

Additional Information

Typing !ready at the start of the match is mandatory, even if other players aren't ready yet. Visit the "Rules" section to familiarize yourself with our guidelines and ensure a fair competitive environment. Go to the "Maps" section to download any required custom maps.
